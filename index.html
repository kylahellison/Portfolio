About Me

Hello, there!

I taught myself how to code back in the days when AOL Instant Messenger, MySpace, and flashy GIFs were some of the hottest trends on the internet. I'd spend hours analyzing code using "View Source" to figure out how everything worked.

When I went off to college, I took my studies in a different direction. Years later, I found myself wishing I had continued coding.

While researching ways that I could get back into coding, I stumbled across teamtreehouse.com. They offer courses in web design, web development, and mobile development. After a couple years of studying on my own, I was accepted into their Front End Web Development TechDegree beta program. The program is designed to teach you the fundamentals of front-end web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, accessibility and user experience.

The projects you see in my portfolio were generated from this curriculum, and showcase all of the things I learned throughout my TechDegree experience.

I am still taking courses on Treehouse to make sure I'm always up to date with the latest in web design. Want to check out my up-to-the-minute Treehouse stats?
